Freshman Year

During freshman year, I wholeheartedly believed I wanted to go into the medical field... I did not.
I was a lot more timid then, but then again, pretty much everyone was. I had a few more friends, since then some of us have moved to different schools or drifted to other friend groups. We all still intermingle, though.
While I wasn't the most adventurous at the time, I did partake in my old middle school's drama show, which sadly got cut off due to covid.
Covid was... interesting. I really struggled to keep myself focused on school, and my language, German, was not at all enjoyable outside of the classroom. I did, however, make tons of online friends on a minecraft server (which has since been shut down), and I'm still quite close with them today.
